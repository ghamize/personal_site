About Me

I see the world as a combination of beauty and operations. I believe we all need a balance of some kind, whether that be in our day-to-day life or in our website designs. I've applied this philosophy to my own life. I began website development and graphic design at the meager age of 9, but made sure I spent some of my time playing outside, too! We all need balance, right?
